On September 13th, NewCo comes to Asia for the first time, planting its feet in Shanghai. NewCo is a new kind of event experience: a mashup of an open studio tour and a business conference, with the vibe of a music festival. There are now 17 cities globally who host the NewCo festival, and over 2500 companies around the world have opened their doors for the event.

"Nothing like NewCo existed in China before, where you get to gather inside the offices of the world’s leading internet companies," says William Bao Bean, Managing Director of Chinaccelerator. "We are very happy to launch Shanghai as the 17th city in the world hosting a NewCo festival."

NewCo’s mission is to identify and celebrate new kinds of companies driving the transition to a sustainable, positive-impact business ecosystem. This is exactly the energy that Chinaccelerator is exuding. The fund behind Chinaccelerator, SOSV, is committed to accelerator founders’ big ideas for positive change, and build communities that provide lasting connections. We cordially invite you to celebrate innovation together on September 13th, 2016.

NewCo是一个国际性创新节日，已在全球16座城市举办，累计参与企业超过2500家。它和一般的商务会议或科技大会不同，它更像一个音乐节，好玩、轻松，但有干货，有收获。节日当天，参与者将跟随NewCo拜访城市中的优秀企业，从拓展业务、寻求合作、招揽贤才、结识人脉到交流学习，参与者各取所需，享乐其中。

这是NewCo第一次进军亚洲，中国加速荣幸地担任其主办方，于9月13日在上海举行。

往期NewCo参与者基本是30-40岁的优秀创业者、管理者及行业先驱。此次NewCo将邀请20家上海优秀企业，这些公司大多是A-D轮，现已确认参加的公司包括网红搜索引擎——Robin8，引爆朋友圈的修图APP——足记，全球最大的互联网服务器管理公司——云络科技，获得沪江投资的语言教学平台——italki，国内领先的团队协作工具——Teambition，等等。

NewCo的愿景是寻找并支持那些对社会有积极影响力、注重持续发展的企业。中国加速和其背后的基金SOSV也秉承同样的信念：投资、加速那些创造正面价值的公司，帮助它们长期发展。在此，我们诚挚邀请您参加NewCo，广交好友，为创新精神开一场狂欢派对。

ABOUT CHINACCELERATOR 关于中国加速

Chinaccelerator, the first startup accelerator in China and Asia, is a mentorship-driven program helping internet startups from around the world cross borders - from the world into China and from China to the world. It is backed by the venture fund SOSV with $250M AUM operating five accelerator programs around the world.

This September, NewCo and Chinaccelerator are coming together to connect the world’s top start-ups, high-growth companies, investors, influencers and entrepreneurs through NewCo Shanghai and Chinaccelerator 8x8, an event where 8 of the tech industries biggest movers and shakers give 8 minute talks about how they have succeeded in building tech companies.

中国加速是亚洲第一个以导师为驱动的软件创业加速器，是管理着2.5亿美元的早期创投SOSV基金投资成立的全球加速器之一。它覆盖全球，每年经营两届创业项目，帮助来自全球的初创公司进入中国市场，也帮助中国公司进入国际市场。中国加速主要关注电子商务、金融科技、数据营销以及教育等生活方式类创业项目。它拥有国际化的背景，集合了来自全球的顶级导师、合作伙伴以及投资人，为创业者提供更广泛的视角和更丰富的资源。
